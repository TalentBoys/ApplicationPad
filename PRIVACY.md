# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 2026**

## Overview

ApplicationPad is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** Specifically:

- No personal information
- No usage analytics
- No crash reports
- No tracking or advertising

## Local Storage

ApplicationPad stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- User preferences (window size, icon size, hotkey settings)
- Recent app usage history

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Permissions

ApplicationPad requires **Accessibility permission** solely to enable the global hotkey feature. This permission is not used for any other purpose.
