Features 功能亮点

Folder Organization 文件夹整理

Create folders to group your apps by category. Drag apps together to organize your launcher. 创建文件夹，按类别归类应用。拖拽应用即可整理你的启动器。

Custom App Ordering 自定义排序

Drag to reorder apps across pages. Your layout is saved and persists between sessions. 跨页面拖拽排序，布局自动保存，下次打开依然保持。

Pinyin Search 拼音搜索

Find apps by typing pinyin initials. Type "wy" to quickly locate Chinese-named apps. 输入拼音首字母即可搜索应用，比如输入"wy"快速找到中文应用。

Customizable Layout 自定义布局

Adjust rows, columns, icon size, and padding to fit your screen and workflow. 自由调整行数、列数、图标大小和间距，适配你的屏幕和工作流。

Global Hotkey 全局快捷键

Launch instantly with a configurable keyboard shortcut. Default: Cmd+Shift+Space. 可自定义全局快捷键，一键呼出。默认：Cmd+Shift+Space。

Recent Apps 最近使用

Frequently used apps are automatically prioritized for quick access. 常用应用自动置顶，快速访问你最需要的工具。

Why ApplicationPad? 为什么选择 ApplicationPad？

Apple removed the classic Launchpad in macOS 26 Tahoe, leaving many users without their favorite app launcher. ApplicationPad is the best Launchpad alternative — it brings back everything you loved about Launchpad and more. Whether you're looking to bring back Launchpad on macOS Tahoe, need a Launchpad replacement for macOS Sequoia, or simply want a better app launcher for Mac, ApplicationPad is the answer.

Unlike the old Launchpad, ApplicationPad gives you full control: custom grid layouts, folder organization, drag-to-reorder across pages, pinyin search for Chinese app names, and a configurable global hotkey. It's the macOS app launcher that Apple should have built.

Works on macOS 14 Sonoma, macOS 15 Sequoia, and macOS 26 Tahoe. Supports both Apple Silicon and Intel Macs. Free to download.
